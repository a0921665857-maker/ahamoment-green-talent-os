# PRODUCT_THESIS.md

## One-liner
AhaMoment Green Talent OS is an AI-native, bilingual (EN / 繁中) talent mobility platform for APAC green-economy professionals. The commercial wedge is **Green Career MRI**: an upload-first diagnostic that converts messy career materials into a precise positioning report — free Lite report → paid human-reviewed services.

## The problem
APAC professionals in ESG, sustainability, climate, carbon markets, green finance, impact investing, climate tech, and sustainability consulting have valuable but messy experience. International MBA programs and global climate employers require clear, credible, commercially framed narratives. The translation layer between the two barely exists, and what exists (overseas study agencies, generic coaches, generic AI tools) is either expensive, generic, or not climate-native.

## Validated demand signal
Michael's own customer discovery (~7 coaching prospects, mid-2026) showed the audience skews toward **career direction confusion** rather than active MBA applicants. Therefore the product front door is career positioning ("Green Career MRI"), with MBA readiness as one lens inside it — not an MBA admissions tool with career questions bolted on. This ordering is a binding product decision.

## Core product insight
**Upload first, ask later.** Users churn when asked to type before seeing value. The system analyzes materials the user already has (CV PDF, LinkedIn paste, notes, prior AI career chats, voice-intro transcript), confirms its understanding, then asks only 3–5 truly missing questions. Effort comes after value.

## Why Michael (founder–market fit)
Big 4 ESG consulting (Deloitte/KPMG, SBTi, CBAM, carbon accounting, VCM/Article 6) → INSEAD MBA → carbon-markets SaaS (APAC enterprise CS). He has personally made the transition the users want to make, in both languages, and can encode the hiring/adcom judgment into the scoring rubrics — which are the IP.

## Business model ladder
1. **Now (V1):** free MRI Lite → paid human services (Teardown USD 249 → Sprints USD 800–2,000 → Full Package USD 3,000–5,000). Cash flow + learning.
2. **Next:** productized AI modules (interviewer, CV/LinkedIn optimizer, MBA story engine) raise margin per user.
3. **Later:** the accumulating taxonomized profile corpus becomes a green talent graph → employer/school-side products, market intelligence, matching.

## Differentiation
- Domain-encoded rubrics (not generic career-quiz logic).
- Bilingual native output, APAC framing, Traditional Chinese done properly.
- Upload-first UX vs questionnaire-first competitors.
- Human escalation path with a credible operator behind it.
- Data architecture that compounds (taxonomy slugs → graph), which consulting never does.

## Non-goals for V1 (binding)
No login, job board, marketplace, community, payment backend, subscriptions, LinkedIn scraping, ChatGPT/Claude memory access, essay-writing automation, employer matching.

## V1 success metrics (beta, first ~6 weeks)
- Start→completed report ≥ 60%
- Completed report → email captured = 100% (email-gated; see CUSTOMER_JOURNEY)
- Report → CTA click ≥ 25%; report → booked call ≥ 8%
- First 3 paid customers within 30 days of beta start
- zh-TW report quality rated acceptable by Michael on all seed profiles (Phase 4)

## Guardrail sentence
Build narrow (one diagnostic, one funnel, two languages), architect broad (taxonomy, canonical profile schema, locale system, prompt contracts).
