# ROADMAP.md

## V1 — Green Career MRI (build target: ~3–4 part-time weeks)
Scope: everything in Phases 3–7 of the brief as refined by Phase 2 docs. Exit gate: 8 golden seeds pass; zh-TW quality approved; privacy checklist green; deployed; 20 beta users invited.

## V1.1 — friction & follow-through (gate: ≥3 paid customers OR 50 completed MRIs)
In-browser voice recording + transcription · Resend email automation (D0/D2/D6) · real Stripe payment links + payments table · report share-image (OG card) · JSON-LD · Supabase Auth for admin · pg_cron purge · admin CSV export polish · first 2 SEO pillar pages per locale.

## V2 — productized modules (gate: ≥US$5k cumulative revenue + clear repeat demand signal)
User accounts + profile versioning (re-run MRI, track deltas) · AI interviewer + question generator (feeds `mock_interview_pack`) · CV/LinkedIn optimizer (paid, bounded) · MBA story engine (assistive, never ghost-writing) · remaining SEO pillar/cluster pages · skill-gap engine v0.

## V3 — graph era (gate: ≥1,000 consented quality snapshots + legal review)
Taxonomy → DB · nodes/edges ETL · aggregate insight reports ("State of APAC Green Talent") · career path simulator from transition data · employer/school partnership pilots (re-consent flow first) · market trend dashboard.

## Deliberately unscheduled
Job board, open marketplace, community/cohorts, recruiter self-serve — each only after the preceding gate proves demand, never before.
