# UX_PRINCIPLES.md

## The ten principles
1. **Upload first, ask later.** Never ask the user to type what their materials already say.
2. **Show understanding before asking anything.** The confirmation page is the product's credibility engine.
3. **Effort after value.** Email and questions come only after the system has demonstrated comprehension.
4. **One decision per screen.** No combined mega-forms.
5. **Honest progress.** Named stages ("Reading your CV…"), never fake percentages, never fake countdowns/scarcity.
6. **Bands, not numbers.** Users see Emerging / Developing / Strong (萌芽 / 發展中 / 紮實). Raw scores are admin-only. Numbers invite arguments; bands invite conversations.
7. **Calm premium, not motivational.** No exclamation marks, no emoji in product UI, no stock-photo optimism, generous whitespace, restrained palette (deep green/ink/off-white direction; finalize in Phase 3 with frontend-design skill).
8. **Specific beats flattering.** Every report section references the user's actual material. Praise without evidence is banned in prompts.
9. **Error empathy.** Failures say what happened, what we kept, and the one action to take. Never blame the input.
10. **Bilingual parity, not translation.** zh-TW is written, not converted. If a line sounds like translated marketing, rewrite it.

## Traditional Chinese craft rules
- Font stack: `"Noto Sans TC", "PingFang TC", "Microsoft JhengHei", sans-serif`; line-height ≥ 1.8 for body.
- No italics (poor CJK support) — use weight or color for emphasis.
- Full-width punctuation（，。：）in zh copy; no mixed half-width commas.
- Half-width spaces around embedded Latin/numbers: 「在 ESG 領域 7 年」.
- Tone register: 專業、冷靜、可信 — closer to 商業顧問 than 補習班. Avoid 「保證」「秒懂」「爆款」-style wording.
- Dates/numbers: report dates as 2026 年 6 月; currency shown as USD with figure (US$249) in both locales.

## Trust surface checklist (landing + flow)
- Founder strip: Big 4 ESG → INSEAD → carbon-markets SaaS, bilingual — facts, not adjectives.
- Privacy line visible before any upload ("your materials are used only to generate your report; auto-deleted from raw storage after 90 days; delete anytime").
- No outcome guarantees anywhere (admissions, salary, offers).
- "What you'll get" preview of the report's section names so the value is concrete pre-upload.

## Anti-patterns (hard bans)
Countdown timers; "only 3 spots left"; testimonial walls without consent; pop-ups; auto-playing video; quiz-style progress gamification; asking for phone numbers in V1.
