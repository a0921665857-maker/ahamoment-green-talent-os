## A. 暖名單外推(功能一:寄信給「30 分鐘聊聊」的人)

**名單怎麼拿**:Calendly / Cal.com 後台匯出 invitees CSV → 用 repo 已有的 Resend 群發(或 Gmail 合併列印)。**推的是氣候職涯產品,不是 MRI。**

### 中文版
**主旨**:還記得我們聊過的那段嗎?做了個小東西想第一個給你試

嗨 {{名字}},

之前跟你聊的那 30 分鐘我印象很深——{{你當時提到想往氣候/永續走}}。

那之後我做了一個東西:一個免費的氣候職涯測評。把你的背景丟進去,它會告訴你最適合切入的綠領路徑,以及怎麼把你現在的專業接上去。

想第一個給你試,因為你的回饋對我最有用。兩個忙擇一就好:
1. 花 3 分鐘玩玩,告訴我哪裡有用、哪裡卡:{{連結}}
2. 身邊有人在想轉氣候,幫我轉給他。

回「最近沒空」也完全 OK。謝謝你 🙏
Michael

### English
**Subject**: That conversation stuck with me — built something, want you to be first

Hi {{name}},

Our 30-minute chat stuck with me — {{you mentioned wanting to move toward climate/sustainability}}.

Since then I built a free climate-career assessment. Drop in your background and it shows the green-collar paths that fit you, and how to bridge your current expertise into them.

I'd love you to be one of the first to try it — your feedback matters most. Either helps:
1. Spend 3 minutes trying it, tell me what's useful / where you got stuck: {{link}}
2. If someone you know is eyeing a move into climate, forward it.

Totally fine to reply "no time." Thank you 🙏
Michael

## B. 導師邀請(功能三:邀 INSEAD / Deloitte / APAC 人脈當導師)

低承諾、講清楚運作、給對方好處。

### 中文版
**主旨**:想邀你當氣候轉職者的導師(很輕量)

嗨 {{名字}},

直接說重點:我在做一個幫人轉進氣候/綠領工作的產品,正找少數幾位真正懂這行的人當導師。第一個想到你——因為 {{你在 Deloitte 永續 / 你在氣候圈的經歷 / INSEAD 同圈}}。

要做的事很輕量:每月一兩次、每次 30 分鐘,線上跟一位認真想轉氣候的人聊聊。篩選、媒合、排程我來,你只要出現、分享真實經驗。

對你的好處:接觸一批高素質、想進這行的人(未來可能是你的同事、團隊或人脈),也等於在幫產業長出人才。

有興趣的話,我們約 15 分鐘把運作講清楚?
Michael

### English
**Subject**: Invite to mentor climate career-switchers (very light touch)

Hi {{name}},

Straight to it: I'm building a product that helps people break into climate / green-collar work, and I'm recruiting a small group of people who actually know the field as mentors. You came to mind first — because {{your sustainability work at Deloitte / your climate experience / INSEAD}}.

It's light: once or twice a month, 30 minutes online with someone serious about moving into climate. I handle vetting, matching, scheduling — you just show up and share what you really know.

What's in it for you: access to a high-quality pool of people entering the field (future colleagues, hires, or network), and you're literally helping grow the industry's talent.

Open to a quick 15-min call so I can explain how it works?
Michael

## C. LinkedIn 找導師:布林搜尋語法

LinkedIn 一般搜尋:**篩選器**(學校 / 地區 / 現職公司 / 產業)+ **關鍵字框用布林**。要更強的篩選與匯出可用 Sales Navigator(~US$100/月,選配)。

**INSEAD 校友、APAC、做永續**
- 篩選:School = `INSEAD`;Location = `Singapore` / `Hong Kong` / `Tokyo` / `Sydney`…
- 關鍵字:`("sustainability" OR "ESG" OR "climate" OR "decarbonization" OR "carbon" OR "net zero")`

**Deloitte(或四大)永續人**
- 篩選:Current company = `Deloitte`(可加 `EY` `KPMG` `PwC`)
- 關鍵字:`("sustainability" OR "climate" OR "ESG" OR "carbon")`

**直接鎖高階/對口職稱、APAC**
- 關鍵字:`("Head of Sustainability" OR "Chief Sustainability Officer" OR "ESG Lead" OR "Sustainability Manager" OR "Decarbonization")`
- 篩選:Location = APAC 各城市

**小技巧**:先用「2nd 度人脈 + 共同 INSEAD」優先,接受率最高;每天約 10–15 位,別一次轟炸。
